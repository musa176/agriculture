These days livestock farmers cannot afford to farm with animals that don’t perform optimally. Thanks to BKB, farmers now have access to information to easily make decisions about their herds.

In the modern era where technology is becoming increasingly relevant in the livestock industry, the big secret is to use the data collected in a feedback cycle for decision making. If you don’t learn from the past, how do you change what you are currently doing?

BKB offers its data collection and processing service to farmers. DSS stands for Decision Support Service, creating the structure for producers to collect data for analysis.
